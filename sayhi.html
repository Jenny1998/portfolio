UX Research and Design | Prototype

Say Hi Full UX Project

SayHi is a free language learning skill of Amazon Echo & Alexa. It allows the users to listen and talk with Alexa in Spanish, French, German, or Danish. It contains several courses for each language, and the users will understand the conversations they heard by the end of each lesson.

Rather than focusing on the reading and writing skills, SayHi skill assists the users to speak and listen. It’s a fun and efficient way to learn a language fast.

This is a comprehensive UX Design Project with research and prototypes. It involves audience analysis, personas, user journey maps, design choices, overall flowchart, wireframes, design panels (with interactivity), usability test, revision and improvement, and a final pitch.
