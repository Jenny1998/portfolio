KitKat Seasonal Flavor Summit

Ad Creative | Copy Writing | Art Direction

This campaign for KitKat is the second part of my first portfolio campaign.

KitKat, as a very popular confectionery treat, became very popular in Japan and many other Asian countries with more than 300 seasonal regional limited flavors.

KitKat also has recently introduced two new flavors in the US Market, green tea and ruby cocoa. This campaign pretends as KitKat is starting to bring more distinctive seasonal flavors into the US.
