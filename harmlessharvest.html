Harmless Harvest Coconut Water

Ad Creative | Copy Writing | Art Direction

My first portfolio ad campaign for my favorite coconut water brand

This Campaign aims to promote Harmless Harvest Coconut Water by introducing its authentic flavor and unparalleled qualities.

Harmless Harvest Organic Coconut Water is the first non-thermally pasteurized coconut water in the US. It’s made from the best-tasting coconuts in the world, grown in Thailand.
It’s currently sold in many large domestic retailers like Whole Foods, Harris Teeter, etc. It also has online delivery services, supported by many online retailers including Amazon.com. The pink coconut water has three package sizes: 8.75oz, $2.99; 16oz, &4.99; 32oz $9.99.
Though its comparatively expensive price makes the product less competitive in the coconut water market, its pink color and authentic flavor attract and retain many consumers.
