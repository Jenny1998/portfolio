Pitching slides for Heavenly Buffaloes

"LEAVE A TARHEEL FOOTPRINT IN THE CHAPEL HILL COMMUNITY AND ITS DIGITAL SPACES.""

Our team of five worked for the local business Heavenly Buffaloes and built up a full advertising campaign involving market research, social media mockup, web and app redesign, and PR strategies. I assumed the responsibility of a strategist, a graphic designer, and a web designer.

We complete the whole campaign including the 16 pages deliverable, the final pitch to the client, and two interactive prototypes within the 24-hr intensive student advertising competition.

Our final pitch received positive feedback from the client and also nine judges from the ad/pr industry. Our work also was taken into consideration by the Heavenly Buffaloes for its future brand development and business expansion.
