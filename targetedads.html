Targeted Ads and Their Impact

Ad Research | Ad Analysis | New Ad Age

Targeted Ads Research Presentation

Surveillance Capitalism. Yes, nowadays growing businesses and tech giants are tracking your online activities and predicting your behaviors. Everything they are doing is for nudging your purchase decisions and generating more profits.

We recognized this trending issue and started our research focusing on the impact of growing targeted ads on our day to day social media platforms and websites. Our Quatrics Survey and Secondary research revealed the facts of how customers are feeling about them and the leading ad tech and social media platforms in the industry.
